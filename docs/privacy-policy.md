# Privacy Policy – Polish✦

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally
identifiable information.

## Permissions

- **storage** – Saves your toggle preferences locally in your browser only.
- **scripting / tabs / sidePanel** – Used to apply visual modifications
  to the active tab. No data is sent externally.

## Third-Party Services

## Third-Party Services

Page content may be sent to the Groq API (running the Llama model) solely to generate
visual enhancement suggestions. No data is stored or shared beyond
this single request. The API integration is fully open-source and auditable at:
https://github.com/Royshell/polish-api

See Groq's privacy policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy
